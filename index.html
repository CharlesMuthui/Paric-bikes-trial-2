Welcome to Paric Bikes!

At, Paric Bikes we believe that the best way to explore the great outdoors is on two wheels. Founded with a passion for adventure and a love for nature, our company offers top-quality bike rentals and thrilling mountain biking hikes for enthusiasts of all skill levels.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an unforgettable outdoor experience by offering reliable bike rentals and guided mountain hikes that showcase the beauty and excitement of the natural world. We are dedicated to promoting a healthy and active lifestyle while ensuring the safety and enjoyment of our customers.
